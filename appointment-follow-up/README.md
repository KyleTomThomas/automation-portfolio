# Appointment Follow-Up Automation

## What it does
Automatically follows up with customers/clients after a scheduled appointment, so no post-appointment step relies on someone remembering to do it manually.

## Workflow
1. **Trigger** – An appointment is marked as completed (or a scheduled time passes) in the booking system/calendar.
2. **Wait/Delay** – The automation waits a set period after the appointment (e.g. same day or next day).
3. **Follow-Up Message** – An automated email/SMS is sent thanking the customer, requesting a review, or offering next steps (rebooking, additional services, etc.).
4. **No-Show Handling** – If the appointment was missed, a different follow-up (reschedule prompt) is triggered instead.
5. **Log/Update** – The CRM or spreadsheet is updated with the follow-up status for reporting.

## Why it matters
Consistent follow-up increases repeat business and reviews, but it's one of the first things that gets skipped when a team is busy. Automating it guarantees every customer gets a timely, consistent touchpoint after their appointment.
