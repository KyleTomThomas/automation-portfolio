# Lead Response Automation

## What it does
Automatically responds to and routes new inbound leads the moment they come in, instead of waiting for someone to manually check a form, inbox, or CRM.

## Workflow
1. **Trigger** – A new lead is captured (e.g. website form submission, ad lead form, or CRM entry).
2. **Enrich/Qualify** – Basic lead details are checked or enriched (source, service interest, contact info).
3. **Instant Response** – An automated email/SMS acknowledgment is sent to the lead within seconds of submission.
4. **Notify** – The sales/owner team is notified in real time (e.g. Slack, email, or text) with the lead details.
5. **Route** – The lead is logged into a CRM or spreadsheet and assigned/tagged for follow-up.

## Why it matters
Fast response time is one of the biggest factors in lead conversion. This automation removes the delay between a lead coming in and first contact, so no lead goes cold waiting on a human to notice it.
